About Folio: Saving & Protecting Lives

A digital health innovation born from the 3MTT Knowledge Showcase Challenge, designed to solve Nigeria's immunization crisis through biometrics, AI, and community education.

Founder & Developer

Ubaida Aliyu

A passionate student at the School of Nouse, Ubaida recognized the devastating impact of Nigeria's immunization gaps. Combining her knowledge of software development with a deep understanding of community health challenges, she created Folio to ensure every child gets their shot at life.

Her vision: a Nigeria where zero-dose children no longer exist, where every birth is registered digitally, and where every vaccine reaches its intended recipient. Folio is her contribution to the 3MTT Knowledge Showcase Challenge and her commitment to saving lives through technology.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision and every line of code in the Folio platform.

💚

Every Life Matters

No child should die from a preventable disease. We believe universal vaccine access is a human right.

📊

Data Integrity

Accurate data saves lives. Our biometric system eliminates fraud, duplicates, and record loss.

🌍

Community First

Solutions must work in the communities they serve. Multilingual, offline-first, and culturally sensitive.

📡

Technology for Good

Using Android, AI, and biometrics not for profit, but to protect the most vulnerable among us.

🤝

Partnership

Collaboration with NPHCDA, WHO, UNICEF, NGOs, and community leaders amplifies our impact.

🎯

SDG 3 Alignment

Folio directly supports UN Sustainable Development Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being for all.
